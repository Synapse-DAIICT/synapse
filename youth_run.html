ABOUT YOUTH RUN

The Youth Run is one of the most unique aspects of Synapse. It is a 7 km marathon in the city of Gandhinagar organised in an attempt to promote ideas that drive the youth of today, social and otherwise and show our support for the same. The Youth Run is a great display of what the youth is passionate about.
This year, Youth Run will be organized on 22nd January, 2017 supporting the issues of Green Gandhinagar, Swachh Gandhinagar and AIDS. The inspiration for the run is to provide the youth an opportunity to voice their opinions on issues that are close to the society, while keeping their health at the centre.
